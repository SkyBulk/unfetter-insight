Why Mandiant Consulting?

Discover how consulting services can help you overcome security challenges and better protect your organization.

Defending against cyber attacks is more difficult than ever. The bad guys are highly sophisticated, well funded, well organized, and highly incentivized. Their tactics, techniques and procedures are constantly evolving. And they’re more persistent than ever.

More and more organizations are finding themselves ill equipped to deal with these threats. Consulting services can level the playing field by providing the experience, expertise, and industry intelligence you need to win the battle against cyber attackers — today and into the future.

Advantages of choosing Mandiant Consulting

Given the rapidly evolving threat landscape, organizations are increasingly challenged to protect their key assets. Mandiant from FireEye is uniquely qualified to help you meet the challenge. We’re the world-leader and number one choice of organizations that need cyber incident response support and cyber security consulting services. No other consultants can offer you what we can.

Expertise fighting evil

  • With over 13 years of front-line incident response experience dealing with advanced threat actors from around the globe, we know how the bad guys think and are better prepared to stop them than anyone else.
  • We work approximately 200,000 hours a year on cyber incident response to the world’s most consequential breaches. That gives us a unique view into how attackers break in and what they do once on the inside.
  • With over 350 consultants in 17 countries across five continents, you can leverage both the technical expertise of on-site consultants and remote services for everything from breach response to proactive cyber security program assessment to staff education.

Respond to cyber incidents with speed, scale, and efficiency

  • We leverage FireEye cloud and on-premise technology to deliver rapid IR services at scale in Microsoft Windows, Linux, and macOS environments to provide you with speedy investigations at minimal expense.
  • Industry-leading research and innovation, pioneering new detection mechanisms and new forensics artifacts enable us to help you identify new threat actors.

Empowering you to protect what matters most

  • We offer you much more than just breach response. Our solutions-focused approach enables you to address your immediate security concerns and helps you better defend against tomorrow’s attackers.
  • Our deep understanding of the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) employed by all types of threat actors around the world allows us to help you more effectively protect against attacks.
  • Mandiant is a trusted advisor to organizations big and small around the world, called on by nearly one third of the Forbes Global 2000.

When you choose Mandiant, you can rest assured knowing that you’ll have the world’s most experienced security experts on your side when you need them most.
